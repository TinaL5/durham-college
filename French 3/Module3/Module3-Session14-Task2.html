Task 2 | Preparing the Meeting Agenda

Welcome!

This task will help you review how to complete several communication tasks.



Learning Goals

  • Describing one’s typical day
  • Making a reservation
  • Indicating one’s way
  • Presenting one’s education
  • Rescheduling an appointment
  • Requesting a day off
  • Writing a project status email
  • Describing a company
  • Describing a product
  • Handling a claim

COMMUNICATE | 15 Minutes

WRITE | A Day in the Kitchen

Write in French at least five sentences to describe a day in the life of a chef.

Feel free to read this article for more information.

Be sure to use the reflexive verbs. Feel free to use a dictionary or a translator.

WRITE | Making a reservation

Imagine that you’d like to make a reservation to a Montréal restaurant, using the information below.

Vous souhaitez réserver une table pour trois personnes, au restaurant La Poutine Fumée situé au bord du Fleuve Saint Laurent à Montréal. Le restaurant offre des tables avec vue sur le fleuve.

Write an email to make your reservation. Be sure to mention the day, time, and number of guests as well as where you would like to be seated.

WRITE | Situating Places

Imagine someone asks you where the following places are:

  1. Centre Eaton
  2. Station de métro
  3. Place des arts
  4. La place du Canada

Using the information provided on the map, write at least 3 sentences to help the person locate the places.



Source: Voyage Montréal

WRITE | Your professional introduction

Answer the following question:

Présentez-vous. Pouvez-vous nous parler de votre parcours académique?

Write up to 100 words to answer this question, using the vocabulary you have learned in this task to talk about one’s education.

WRITE | Rescheduling an apointment

Imagine you have a meeting with your colleague Thomas, on Thursday December 23 at 4 pm, but something came up and you will have to go pick up your children.

Write an email to ask your colleague if the meeting can be rescheduled.

Write up to 4 sentences to complete this task.

WRITE | Requesting a Day off

Imagine you have to renew your driver’s license and your health card.

Write an email to ask your manager, Tao, if you can take your morning off to go to Service Canada.

Write up to 4 sentences to answer this question.

WRITE | Providing a Project Status

Complete the following task.

Draft an email to provide a status to Mme Coulibaly about the renovation work in her new office space.

Write at least 5 sentences in French using the information below:

  • Destinataire: Mme Coulibaly
  • Statut :
    Peinture – complété
    Installation du parquet – en cours
    Livraison des meubles – le 25 mai
    Fin des travaux – le 30 mai

WRITE | Describing a Company

Complete the following task:

Choose a start-up from the Top Canadian Startups To Watch 2021 list.

Write in French at least 10 sentences to describe the start up.

Be sure to mention the following information:

  • Nom
  • Secteur d'activité
  • Spécialisation
  • Année de fondation
  • Nombre d'employés
  • Développement

Remember to include the vocabulary of this session. Feel free to use a dictionary or a translator.

SPEAK | Describing a Product

Your turn!

Imagine that you have to describe your own smartphone.

Use the information below to list its main features and describe how you use it.

Principales caractéristiques

  • La taille
  • Le poids
  • La capacité
  • L’âge
  • L’utilisation

Your task:

Record an audio of your description.

WRITE | Handling a Claim

Imagine that you are Éric.
Your colleague Murielle is waiting for the new computers of four of her team members.
Write an email to Murielle to apologize and explain that the computers have been delayed due to a delivery issue.
Offer to have the computers installed on Friday.

Be sure to include the vocabulary of this session. Feel free to use a dictionary or a translator.
