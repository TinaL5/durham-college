Summary and Next Week

Summary

The summary section provides students with a quick summary of the key learning outcomes they should have learned from this module or topic.

Student Note:

It is best practice to prepare your own study notes at the end of each week. If you do this weekly, it will help reinforce key concepts and serve as a study aid in cases where there are tests/exams.

Next Week

This is where you can provide a brief snapshot of what students will be doing next week. You may also want to use this section as a reminder of what your students will need to have completed for next week.

For Example:

Next week we will focus on...

  • Include any questions you want your students to be able to answer at the start of your topic
  • Include any due dates for assignments that are due in next week’s time-period
