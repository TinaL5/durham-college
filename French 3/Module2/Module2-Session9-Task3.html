GRAMMAR | Have someone do something

How to express the idea that we have someone doing something?

You can use the structure Faire + verb to express the idea that you have someone doing something.

Expressing the Idea of Having Someone Do Something
Active Reflexive

Sujet + faire (conjugated) + infinitive of a verb + object

Sujet + reflexive pronoun + faire (conjugated) + infinitive of a verb + object

Je fais réparer ma voiture.
Je fais étudier mes enfants.

Je me fais couper les cheveux.
Tu te fais livrer ton déjeuner.
