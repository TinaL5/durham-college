VOCABULARY | Creating a Voicemail Greeting Message

Below is an example of a voicemail greeting message.

Bonjour, vous avez joint la messagerie de Nicolas Dupuis (1). Je suis indisponible pour le moment. Veuillez laisser un message indiquant la raison de votre appel (2). Je vous rappellerai dans le plus brefs délais (3). Merci.

  1. Start by giving your name.
  2. Use this sentence to politely ask people to indicate their reason for calling.
  3. Indicate when people can expect you to return their call.

VOCABULARY | Leaving a Message

Below are some key phrases to leave a voicemail message.

Se présenter
French English

Bonjour, Nicolas Dupuis.

Hello, Nicolas Dupuis here.

Bonjour, Murielle Paquette de l’entreprise Unicom.

Hello, Murielle Paquette from Unicom.
Giving the reason for calling me
French English

J’appelle afin d’avoir plus d’information sur…

I am calling to get more information about...

Je vous contacte concernant l’offre (d’emploi, de stage, de formation) sur vote site Internet.

I am contacting you regarding the (job, internship, training) offer on your website.

Je vous rappelle suite à votre courriel/ votre appel du 20 novembre.

I am calling you back following your email/call of November 20.

Tel que convenu, je vous rappelle afin de vous confirmer mes disponibilités.

As agreed, I will call you back to confirm my availability.
Leaving contact details
French English

Vous pouvez me joindre au 5-1-4-9-8-6-3-0-0-0.

You can reach me at 5-1-4-9-8-6-3-0-0.

Merci de me rappeler à ce numéro, pour plus d’information.

Please call me at this number for more information.

Vous pouvez m’envoyer votre demande à l’adresse électronique suivante : murielle.paquette@email.com

You can send your request to the following e-mail address: murielle.paquette@email.com

Pourriez-vous me rappeler au 5-1-4-9-8-6-3-0-0-0?

Could you call me back at 5-1-8-7-8-2-3-0-4-0?

SPEAK | Your voicemail message

Complete the following task.

Imagine that you have to record your voicemail message in French for your business.

Prepare your message and be sure to include:

  • Your name
  • The name of your company (or the company you work for)
  • A brief invitation to leave a message
  • When you expect to return the call

SPEAK | Leaving a message

Complete the following task.

Imagine that you call a gym. You would like to speak to your personal trainer Frédéric to change the dates of your personalized workout program. You agreed to meet with him to train on Mondays, Wednesdays, and Fridays, but you would like to train on Tuesdays instead of Mondays.

Record the voicemail you would leave in French. Be sure to include the following information:

  • Your name
  • The person you are calling
  • Time and date you are calling
  • A clear and concise statement of purpose/action requested
  • Your phone number. Say it twice, slowly
  • Thank the caller and close the message
