Task 2 | Introducing Yourself and People

Welcome!

This task will help you learn how to describe your education.

Learning Goals

  • Introduce yourself to a colleague or an acquaintance
  • Use the present tenses adequately

CONNECT | 15 Minutes

WARM UP | Vocabulary Brainstorming

Take a few minutes to write the words you already know to introduce yourself. This will help you memorize your vocabulary of this task.

WATCH | Presenting One's education

Select the play button to watch this video. You are not expected to understand every word of the video.

Kindly focus on the sentences you can use to present yourself and your education.

UNDERSTAND | First Listening

Now that you have watched the conversation, answer the questions below.

Vrai ou Faux. Choisissez la bonne réponse.

UNDERSTAND | Second Listening

Write in French at least four sentences to summarize what you have learned about Othmane Loufti’s education.

Be sure to include the vocabulary of this session. Feel free to use a dictionary or a translator.

Think about these elements:

  • Where did he study?
  • What type of diploma does he hold?
  • In what institution is he studying now?
  • What degree is he preparing for currently?

Expressions to talk about one's education

J'ai une license en droit.

I have an undergraduate law degree.

Je prépare un certificat en communication.

I’m completing a certificate in communication.

Je suis une formation en informatique.

I’m completing a training program in IT.

J’ai obtenu un diplôme de marketing.

I hold a degree in marketing.
