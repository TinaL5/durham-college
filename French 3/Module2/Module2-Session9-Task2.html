VOCABULARY | Expressing Obligations

When consulting a doctor for a specific condition, we may receive a treatment that we have to take.
This is an obligation. There are two ways to express an obligation in French.

Personal obligation

If it is an obligation that comes from the subject, we use the verb Devoir.

Je ne me sens pas bien, je dois aller chez le médecin.

External obligation

If the obligation does not come from the subject, we use the verb Falloir.

Ma fille ne se sent pas bien, il faut que je l'amène chez le médecin.
