Take a few minutes to write the words you already know to talk about food. This will help you memorize the vocabulary of this task.

Here are a few French verbs to help : “acheter – to buy, commander – to order, manger – to eat, boire – to drink, cuire – to cook, cuisiner – cook or prepare food, laver – to rinse, couper – to cut”

GRAMMAR | Using the Impératif

How to Form It?

The Impératif is formed with only three persons, You, We and You (plural). To form it, we omit the subject of the verb and we use the présent simple conjugation, as shown below:
