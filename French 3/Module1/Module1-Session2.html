Module 1 Daily Living | Session 2

Session 2 Overview

Welcome to session 2. This session includes a formative assessment that will help you prepare for the summative assessment, at the end of module one Daily Living.

Understanding Your Formative Assessment

The assessment consists in the written and video assignments described below.

  • Prepare a 2-minute video where you give instructions on how to cook a simple recipe (300 words).
  • Write a short email to present the recipe (150 words).

Learning Goals

In this assessment your goal is to:

  • Give step by step instructions
  • Use the present tense, the future and the imperative
  • Use adjectives
  • Use time adverbs to sequence steps

2

3

When to use the Futur simple?

To talk about future events

To express future projects

To make assumptions about the future
