12- or 24-hour clock?

Oftentimes, in francophone cultures, the 24-hour clock is used. In Canada, the 12-hour clock is also frequently used. You can indicate the time of day with the following expressions.

It's ten in the morning.

It’s ten in the morning.

It’s noon.

Il est minuit.

It's quarter after ten.

It’s ten in the morning.

It’s quarter to ten.
