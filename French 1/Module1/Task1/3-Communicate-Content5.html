French 1: Module 1 - Communicate

Respond verbally and in writing

Ungraded Formative Feedback from Facilitator

The ungraded formative feedback on this learning activity is meant to guide the learning process and to prepare you for the formative and summative assessments. The facilitator will provide feedback on evaluation criteria pertinent to the task from the same rubric that will be used for the summative assessment. The submission of this activity to the facilitator is mandatory, and only one submission will be presented to the facilitator.

Learning Goals

In this assessment your goal is to:

  • Demonstrate reading and listening comprehension through writing.
  • Contextualise your learning through a task representing a formal situation.

Rubric

Pertinent criteria within this rubric will be used to provide ungraded formative feedback from the facilitator for this task within the module.

Activité: Bonjour!

Steps

1
Read the scenario-based prompt for the activity.

2
Respond in writing to the prompt of the activity.

3
Submit your response in the dropbox called Formative Feedback, Module 1, Task 1: Dropbox Name for feedback from the facilitator.

1 Read the scenario-based prompt for the activity.

Vous trouvez une note sur votre bureau:



2 Respond in writing to the prompt of the activity.

Complétez le dialogue où vous allez à la réunion. N’oubliez pas d’inclure: les salutations, les expressions de courtoisie...

Dialogue prompt 1 Feedback Sample solution
Alex: Bonjour! Comment allez-vous?

Did you include a greeting?

 Bonjour, Alex.

Did you respond with a declarative statement?

Are you maintaining a positive tone?

Je vais très bien.

Are you extending courtesy by asking how Alex is doing?

Did you respond formally with vous?

Comment allez-vous?

Audio recorder: Record yourself saying the suggested dialogue and then listen to check your pronunciation.

Dialogue prompt 2 Feedback Sample solution
Alex: Pas mal. Je reviens dans cinq minutes.

Did you respond with an interrogative statement?

Did you respond formally with vous?

Où allez-vous?

Dialogue prompt 3 Feedback Sample solution
Alex: Je cherche mes lunettes.

Did you respond with a declarative statement?

Did you respond with a positive tone?

Bon.

Did you close the conversation?

À bientôt.
