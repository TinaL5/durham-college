French 1: Module 1

Introduction

Bienvenue! Welcome to the module on Interpersonal Relationships and Communication Strategies! In this module we are learning about how to introduce ourselves and greet people in both formal and informal contexts. We will be able to share contact information as well as converse with friends about everyday activities as they share their preferences. The learning activities connect to the real world through task-based scenarios. This is important because it enables us to communicate in a natural and functional manner.

Learning Objectives and Tasks

Listening, speaking, reading, and writing are integral to language learning and are practised together to reflect authentic communication contexts. The objective of this module is to develop the following skills.

  • Greet colleagues and friends through speaking and writing.
  • Use and respond to courtesy formulas in conversational exchanges.
  • Introduce yourself in formal and everyday situations through speaking and writing.
  • Respond to short texts through speaking and writing.
  • Write sentences using structures to convey formal and everyday messages.
  • Apply appropriate tone in conversation.

Skills are supported with knowledge and strategies and developed by conversing, connecting, and communicating through the following tasks.

  • Greet colleagues
  • Make introductions
  • Converse with friends
