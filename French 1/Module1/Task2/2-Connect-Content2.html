Vowels

In French, there are 5 vowels. They are: a, e, i, o, u

A

as in 'ami'

The 'a' sounds like the 'a' in party.

E

as in 'le'

The 'e' sounds like 'ugh'.

I

as in 'ici'

The 'i' sounds like the 'i' in kite.

O

as in 'pot'

The 'o' sounds like the 'o' in told.

U

as in 'tu'

The 'u' sounds like the 'u' in mute.

Accents
