Aimer and Détester

Aimer means to like / to love
Détester means to hate

Both Aimer and Detester are regular ‘er verbs. The conjugation of these verbs follow the normal conjugation pattern of regular ‘er’ verbs.

AIMER TO LIKE / TO LOVE

I like / I love
(informal)

You like / You love

He or she likes / He or she loves / We like/love

We like / We love
(formal and singular plural)

You like / You love

They like / They love



DÉTESTER TO HATE

I hate
(informal)

You hate

He or she hates / We hate

We hate
(formal and singular/ plural)

We hate

They hate
