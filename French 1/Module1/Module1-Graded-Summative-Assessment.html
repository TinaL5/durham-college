Graded Summative Assessment

As a competency-based course, the course’s overall pass/fail assessment will be based solely on the three module summative assessments, including the summative assessment for this module. The summative assessment integrates groups of tasks, which the student will be required to complete to demonstrate their mastery of the course competency.

For the summative assessment, learners will interact with authentic communication forms (invitations, voice messages, emails, etc.). Learners will receive scenario-based prompts targeting listening and reading and will respond by speaking and writing.

This summative assessment evaluates the module 1 development indicator: 

  • Apply basic listening, speaking, reading and writing communication strategies in formal and everyday situations to support interpersonal relationships.

Learning Goals

In this assessment your goal is to:

  • Demonstrate comprehension through listening and reading.
  • Write and respond verbally to an integrated group of tasks.
  • Contextualise your learning through tasks representing formal and everyday situations.
