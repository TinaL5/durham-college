Ungraded Formative Assessment

The ungraded formative assessment is meant to guide the learning process and to prepare you for the summative assessment. The facilitator will provide feedback on evaluation criteria from the same rubric that will be used for the summative assessment. The formative assessment is mandatory, and only one submission will be presented to the facilitator.

For the formative assessment, learners will interact with authentic communication forms (invitations, voice messages, emails, etc.). Learners will receive scenario-based prompts targeting listening and reading and will respond by speaking and writing.

This formative assessment evaluates the module 1 development indicator:

  • Apply basic listening, speaking, reading and writing communication strategies in formal and everyday situations to support interpersonal relationships.

This assessment can be found by navigating to Activities → Quizzes

Learning Goals

In this assessment your goal is to:

  • Demonstrate comprehension through listening and reading.
  • Write and respond verbally to an integrated group of tasks.
  • Contextualise your learning through tasks representing formal and everyday situations.

Rubric

The rubric will be used to provide ungraded feedback from the facilitator as formative assessment for this module.
