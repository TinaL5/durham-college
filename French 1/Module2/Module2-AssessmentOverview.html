Understanding your Summative Assessment for this Module

The summative assessment consists of six activities, two activities per each of the three module tasks. The activities are presented within a formal or everyday context. You will respond orally or in writing to listening and/or reading prompts. Background information will be provided for each of the activity prompts.

Learning Goals

In this assessment your goal is to:

  • Demonstrate comprehension through listening and reading.
  • Write and respond verbally to an integrated group of tasks.
  • Contextualise your learning through tasks representing formal and everyday situations.

Rubric

This rubric will be used to grade the summative assessment for this module. You will have an opportunity to practice and receive feedback from the facilitator with this rubric in the form of ungraded formative assessment activities prior to submitting your work for the summative assessment.

Notes

Remember:

  • Familiarize yourself with the rubric and the steps of the assessment.
  • To ensure your success, complete all learning activities in the main sections, before starting your summative assessment.
