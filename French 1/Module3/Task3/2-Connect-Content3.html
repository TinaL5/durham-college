L'impératif et la négation

The Imperative

This is used when making a request or giving a command. The imperative is usually formed by using the tu, vous or nous forms of the present tense without the use of the subject pronoun.
Please note that when constructing the imperative, the 's' in the 'tu' form of the 'er' verb is dropped.

For example:

Finis ton repas!

Finish your meal!

Parle plus fort, s'il te plaît!

Speak louder please!

Attendez une minute, s'il vous plaît!

Wait a minute please!


La negation

Ne...pas - Not

In order to construct sentences with a negative structure. We should place ne before the verb and pas after it.

For example:

Affirmative Sentence: Je porte - I am wearing...
Negative Sentence: Je ne porte pas - I am not wearing...

Je vais au travail. - I am going to work.
Je ne vais pas au travail. - I am not going to work.

In the example above, we see the verb 'aller' being used. Ne goes before the conjugated part of the verb 'aller' then pas comes right after.
