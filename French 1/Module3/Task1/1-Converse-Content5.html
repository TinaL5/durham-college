Constructing sentences through structures | Adjectives

The adjective is used to provide details about a noun or a pronoun.

For example: A big bag, a wide road.

In French, adjectives agree in number and gender with the noun and pronoun they modify. Most times, it is necessary to add an e to the masculine adjectives in order to form the feminine.

Let us have a look.

Agreement of adjectives in number (singular and plural)

A. In order to make adjectives plural, in most cases, you can add an s to the masculine and feminine forms.

For example:

grand - grands

grande - grandes

B. Most masculine singular adjectives ending in –al have plural forms ending in –aux.

For example:

Un problème social- des problèmes sociaux
