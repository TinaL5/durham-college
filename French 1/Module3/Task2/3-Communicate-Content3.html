ADJECTIVES

Most French adjectives follow the noun they modify.

For example:
C’est une femme intelligente.
C’est une maison blanche.

Exception: There are a few adjectives which refer to beauty, age and size usually precede the noun.

C’est un grand hotel.
J’ai un nouveau travail.
