Changing Course and Page Banners

This page demonstrates how to change your Course Home Banner and Page Banners using pre-sized images designed for these areas.

Images make content easier to understand for many people, just remember to use images that are meaningful to the content and use succinct alternative text ("alt text") for accessibility. You can find more accessibility resources through The Learning Portal.

Changing Banners

Your course has a picture icon on the Courses Homepage. This Course Homepage Banner is updated through your course options. In addition to the Course Homepage Banner, each content page begins with a full width banner image. This page banner is updated through the HTML Editor.
