French 1: Module 1 - Connect

Connect

A purpose of learning a language is to apply it. Learning by doing can help consolidate and extend content from the previous submodule Converse. You’ll demonstrate your understanding by responding in activities that involve interaction, decision-making, and interpretation. The learning activities simulate real-life applications by connecting to authentic experiences in French.
