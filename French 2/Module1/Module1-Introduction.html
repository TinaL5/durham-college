French 1: Module 1

Introduction

Bienvenue! Welcome to the module on Interpersonal Relationships and Communication Strategies! Throughout this module, we are going to be learning about social interactions, daily and formal conversations, and the expression of feelings. A large variety of task-based activities will allow you to put your skills into practice in an everyday context and in a professional environment. Listening to dialogues, reading texts, and watching videos will provide you the opportunity to engage with the French language and acquire skills through authentic situations.

Learning Objectives and Tasks

Listening, speaking, reading, and writing are integral to language learning and are practised together to reflect authentic communication contexts. The objective of this module is to develop the following skills.

  • Open and close conversational exchanges in formal and informal situations through speaking and writing.
  • Use and respond to courtesy formulas in conversations.
  • Apply appropriate tone in conversation.
  • Express sentiments through speaking and writing.
  • Respond to short texts through speaking and writing.
  • Write sentences using structures to convey formal and everyday messages.
  • Decode and encode using phonetics and spelling conventions.

Skills are supported with knowledge and strategies and developed by conversing, connecting, and communicating through the following tasks.

  • Open and close conversational exchanges
  • Identify and apply appropriate tone
  • Express sentiments
