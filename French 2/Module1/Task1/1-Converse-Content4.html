Construisez les phrases avec les structures

A complete sentence contains at least a subject and a verb. To ask a question, you can invert the verb and subject pronoun. Inversions are frequently used for questions related to greetings.

Vous pouvez is inverted to pouvez-vous to ask a question Pouvez-vous venir?

  • Inversions do not usually apply the subject pronoun je.
  • If the 3rd person singular verb does not end in -d or –t, then a -t is placed between the verb and the subject: Comment va-t-elle?
