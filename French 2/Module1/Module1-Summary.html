Learning Goals

Ensure you have completed the following by the end of this module:

  • Familiarize yourself with the summative assessment rubric.
  • Read all module pages including the videos, activities, etc.
  • Complete all the activities provided, which support achieving competency.
  • Participate in optional online discussions.
  • Participate in optional online conversations with francophone students from La Cité.
  • Complete and submit the ungraded formative assessment.
  • Once the facilitator returns your formative assessment, complete and submit the graded summative assessment.

Supplementary Resources

These optional resources can supplement and extend your learning with extra opportunities to practice French.

  • Duolingo French focuses on real-life communication and offers free lessons to practice listening, speaking, reading, and writing. Practice in pronunciation is included.
  • Mauril is an all-free platform that integrates authentic content from Radio-Canada, such as news, comedy, and documentaries. Mauril uses an approach based on the Canadian Language Benchmarks.
  • Apprendre le français by TV5 Monde offers free exercises through their website or mobile app Apprendre avec TV5MONDE. Filter your search by level A1 débutant for beginner content. Première classe provides free video-based lessons for beginners that highlight French international culture, grammar, and vocabulary.
  • Liberté is a first-year college textbook that is an open educational resource.
  • Office québécoise de la langue française offers vocabulary lists on themes varying from health to technology.
