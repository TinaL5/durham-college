It is important to introduce yourself in a friendly and approachable way at the beginning of each course. Just as you would in a face-to-face classroom, your biography should be informative and provide insight into your personality.

While we highly encourage you to create a welcome video and post it in this space, at a minimum we ask that you type out a short biography as well as include a photograph of yourself. Ensure you write in the first person.

If you are comfortable with creating videos, it is also suggested that you create and post a walkthrough of the course outline to clear up any confusion for students.

The following must be included in your biography:

  1. Relevant education.
  2. Relevant experience.
  3. Something personal such as hobbies, favourite travel spots, types of pets etc. This shows that you are personable but also maintains your privacy and safety.
  4. Turnaround time: I will be checking and responding to your email and discussion board questions and comments within 2 days, and will return submitted assignments within 5 business days after the official assignment due date. If I will be away for more than 48 business hours, I will post a notice with details. This is a good piece of advice for everyone. If you are going to be away, or have other issues that prevent you from participating, please contact me immediately, and let your group know if you are doing group work.
  5. Your Durham College email address: xxx@durhamcollege.ca

Please do not share information such as your private phone number, personal social media sites such as Facebook, or home address.
